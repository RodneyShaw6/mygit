## Flappy Bird Game üê¶

A fun and challenging Flappy Bird game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript!

### How to Play

1. Open `flappy-bird.html` in your web browser
2. Press **SPACE** or **CLICK** to make the bird flap and start the game
3. Navigate through the pipes without hitting them
4. Try to get the highest score possible!

### Game Features

- Smooth physics and animations
- Procedurally generated pipes
- Score tracking
- Game over and restart functionality
- Responsive controls (keyboard and mouse)
- Beautiful gradient backgrounds

### Controls

- **SPACE** or **ENTER** or **CLICK**: Make the bird flap/jump
- The bird will fall due to gravity - keep flapping to stay airborne!

### Tips

- Timing is everything - don't flap too much or too little
- Stay calm and find your rhythm
- The pipes get challenging quickly - good luck!
